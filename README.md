## Key Sections

### Dashboard
Your main workspace showing all projects with statistics and quick access to everything you need.

### Favorites
Quick access to your most important projects. Mark any project as favorite for instant access.

### Recent
View and access your recently opened projects sorted by activity.

### Terminal
Global terminal for running commands across your workspace.

### Scripts
View and manage all scripts from all your projects in one centralized location.

### Settings
Customize scan depth, language preferences, update settings, and more.
